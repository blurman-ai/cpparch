## Methodology

### Graph ‚Äî per-commit, all commits since May 2025
- Tool: `experiments/ai_repo_run/generate_per_commit_graph_drift.py` (already exists).
- Mechanism: `archcheck --diff <sha>~1..<sha> .` over **first-parent** history. Cheap
  (diff of one commit, not a re-scan of the tree).
- "Graph errors" metric: cycles / cyclic SCC + drift signals (added/removed edges,
  shortcut edges, cycle growth).
- Output: `.jsonl` (all commits) + `.md` (non-zero drift only).

### Copy-paste ‚Äî at HEAD, find the culprit on demand
- Step 1: `archcheck --duplication <repo>` at **HEAD** (after vendor-exclude, commit e55d0fe).
  Metric: number of reported copy-paste pairs + whole-file clone count.
- Step 2: **if dup ‚âÝ 0** ‚Üí for interesting pairs, find the **culprit commit**: `git blame`
  over the fragment's lines (or bisect), classify that commit AI/non-AI.
- Per-commit dup over the whole history we **do NOT** do (checkout+scan of each = weeks). Only
  a targeted drill-down on repos with findings.

### AI detection ‚Äî both signals
- Marker list source: `generate_per_commit_structural_drift.py` ‚Üí `AI_MARKERS`.
- Signal 1 (message/trailer): `co-authored-by: claude|cursor|codex|copilot`,
  `noreply@anthropic.com`, `cursor agent`, `claude code`, `ai-assisted`, `generated by ai`,
  `codex-`, `cursor/`, `copilot`, ‚Ä¶
- Signal 2 (author): name/email looks like a bot (`bot`, `[bot]`, `noreply`).
- AI commit = any signal fired.

## Key decisions

### Session 2026-06-05
- **AI signal hierarchy:** trailer > bot-authorship > AI% by messages ‚â´ velocity-ramp (junk ‚Äî
  catches any spike, the top ramp gave AI%=0). **Commit-message length** is a trailer-independent proxy,
  catches invisible IDE-AI and stripped trailers.
- **Giants with review/contributors ‚Äî discard** (debt from size, FP); **vendor for dup ‚Äî mandatory
  to filter** (otherwise esys/T5ynth are falsely "copy-paste champions").
- **2 years of history √ó labeled AI barely overlap** ‚Äî agentic-ness is a 2025 phenomenon; old repos =
  human or token bot-PRs. cajeta (2022‚Üí2026) is a rare exception, the only clean arc.
- **The demo thesis is not "AI caused debt" but "here is agentic C++ without a guard + structural
  debt that the tool surfaces"** ‚Äî causality is not provable, specimens are.

### Original
- **Per-commit graph drift: first-parent only** ‚Äî 280k commits in ~4 hours instead of weeks with full history
- **Duplication at HEAD + blame drill-down** ‚Äî quickly find the current state, then catch the culprit on demand
- **Bimodal distribution of AI%** ‚Äî not a continuum but two peaks: "AI-native" (100%) and "AI-augmented" (50-90%)
- **Does AI% correlate with drift?** ‚Äî graphillion_kyotodd (99% AI ‚Üí 101 graph errors) vs fox1245_NeoGraph (94% AI ‚Üí 114 graph errors) ‚Äî possible correlation, Spearman needed
