## Hypotheses to Test

1. **AI adoption ↔ Project novelty:** Forked/new projects show higher AI%, traditional projects lower
2. **AI adoption ↔ Copy-paste risk:** High AI% might correlate with more duplication candidates (needs graph-drift data)
3. **"AI-native vs AI-augmented":**
   - 100% AI: likely generated entire project in one batch
   - 50-90% AI: selective/incremental AI assistance
   - 0% AI: zero automation, manual engineering only
4. **Scale-independent:** Large projects (1M+ LOC) CAN be 100% AI-generated (CnC example)
